Invoice Period Analysis Overview: Creation vs. Payment Periods

About Invoice Comparison Overview

This section allows you to compare invoices based on two distinct date ranges:

  1. Creation Period: Select the date range during which invoices were created.
  2. Payment Period: Choose the date range when these invoices were paid.

The comparison will show which invoices were created within the selected creation period but remained unpaid, and which of these were paid within the chosen payment period. This helps in analyzing and tracking invoice status and payment trends.
